Welcome to the Prepared Partners LLC blog — your go-to resource for practical, no-nonsense guidance on emergency action plans, fire prevention, OSHA compliance, and emergency management.

Whether you found us through a Google search, a referral, or you're already a client — this blog exists for one reason: to give you the information you need to protect your organization before something forces you to.

**What You'll Find Here**

We publish articles in two main categories:

**Fire Prevention** covers fire code compliance, insurance loss control requirements, evacuation planning, fire hazard assessments, and everything related to keeping your facility fire-safe and your carrier satisfied.

**Emergency Management** covers Emergency Action Plans, Incident Action Plans, tabletop exercises, full-scale drills, OSHA requirements, and the broader world of organizational emergency preparedness.

**How to Find What You Need**

At the top of the blog page you'll find filter buttons — use them to narrow posts by category. Looking for something specific? Use the search bar to find articles by keyword. Every post is labeled with its category and estimated read time so you can decide quickly whether it's relevant to you.
